Note! New Learning Module feature: Quizlets!
Learning module and quiz have been combined. Short "Quizlets" are distributed throughout the module. We think you'll find this format much easier to use, but here are some important tips!

  1. Quizlet Instructions:
    The Quizlets are similar to the longer quizzes you are used to. Answer each question, then click "Score my Quiz." You will be shown the total points you've just earned and have a chance to read your feedback. The final score is not shown until you have finished all the quizlets. When you are done reading feedback, click the big "Record my Score" to continue the rest of the module.
  2. Keeping score:
    At any time, you may click the "Summary and Score" button in the left navigation bar. You will see a list of all the quizlets and any scores you have received so far.
  3. If you do not like the score you've received on any quizlet, you may try it again! Scores are not final until you have taken every quizlet and clicked the last "Send my Final Score to LMS " button in the last module.
  4. Finishing:
    After you have finished all quizzes and read the module, click "Summary and Score" again. You will be shown the final score, and given a chance to print your results. Then click the "Send My Final Score to LMS " button.
